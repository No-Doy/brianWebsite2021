Daft Punk Helmet

Built a Daft Punk Thomas Bangalter helmet equipped with 256 LEDs, a Raspberry Pi Zero W, 10 NeoPixel LEDs (RGB capabilities), and two HT16K33 I2C integrated matrix boards.

Reduced the number of LED connections to the single-board computer by 99% by utilizing the I2C protocol across 256 LEDs and two matrix boards.
